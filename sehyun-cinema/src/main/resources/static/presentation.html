Database Design

ERD · 데이터베이스 설계

members

  • id, username, password
  • name, email, phone
  • grade (일반/VIP/VVIP/LVIP)
  • total_spent, role
  • birth_date, created_at

movies

  • id, title, genre
  • poster_url, trailer_url
  • status (SHOWING/UPCOMING)
  • rating_avg, booking_rate
  • rank_position, age_rating

bookings

  • id, member_id, movie_id
  • theater_type, seat_info
  • adult_count, teen_count
  • total_price, discount_amount
  • payment_method, status

movie_hearts

  • id, member_id, movie_id
  • created_at
  • UNIQUE (member_id, movie_id)

notices + faqs

  • id, title, content
  • category, is_important
  • view_count, created_at

inquiries

  • id, member_id
  • category, inquiry_type
  • title, content
  • email_notify, sms_notify
